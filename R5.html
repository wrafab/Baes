Rules and Limiations for the MakerBot Replicator 5th Gen printers


First, it shouldn't be too small. if your model or some parts of your model are too small for the printer to handle,
your printer will be covered in so many supports or fail to print it at all. Second, your model can't have more than one
material if you are using the MakerBot Replicator. Third, almost every additive manufacturing machine can't build a model
without layers. In other words, your model will have layers and you should smooth them after you print it.
