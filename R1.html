Rotation 1: Design challenges & HTML



Marble Challenge



we and other groups were given marbles along with paper, tape, straws, paper clips, and scissors.
Our challenge was to make a single pathway using given materials for a marble to land on a certain
spot. Longer the distance, higher the score, but if the marble couldn't land on the spot, it was
disqualified.


So our group made one long and thin paper pathway for the marble. We had the longest pathway among
the groups, but it was too unstable. I knew we were going to be disqualified, and we were. However,
no one ever made their marbles land on the spot, so it didn't matter regardless.


Maybe if I had one more chance to do this challenge, I would more focus on making more stable and
precise path so that I can actually land on the spot
